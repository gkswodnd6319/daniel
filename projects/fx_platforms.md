## Detailed Breakdown

### 1. Korean Major Banks (Traditional)

Korean banks quote a "cash buying/selling rate" or a "telegraphic transfer rate" that includes a spread over the mid-market (매매기준율) rate. The standard spread is called the 환전 수수료 (exchange commission), expressed as a percentage of the mid-market rate.

| Bank | Standard Spread (USD) | Mobile App 우대율 | Effective Spread After 우대 | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **하나은행 (Hana)** | 1.75% (cash) / 1.0% (transfer) | Up to 90% 우대 (mobile/online) | ~0.175% (cash) / ~0.10% (transfer) | Most aggressive on FX 우대율. "하나 모바일 환전" frequently offers 90% 우대 for USD. |
| **신한은행 (Shinhan)** | 1.75% / 1.0% | Up to 50–70% 우대 (SOL app) | ~0.525–0.875% / ~0.30–0.50% | SOL app provides 우대 coupons; rates vary by campaign. |
| **KB국민은행 (Kookmin)** | 1.75% / 1.0% | Up to 50–80% 우대 (KB Star app) | ~0.35–0.875% / ~0.20–0.50% | KB Star Banking regularly runs 우대 events. 80% 우대 available during promotions. |
| **우리은행 (Woori)** | 1.75% / 1.0% | Up to 70–80% 우대 | ~0.35–0.525% / ~0.20–0.30% | WON Banking app offers competitive 우대. |
| **NH농협은행 (NongHyup)** | 1.75% / 1.0% | Up to 50–70% 우대 | ~0.525–0.875% / ~0.30–0.50% | NH Smart Banking; typically slightly less competitive. |

**Key distinction: Cash vs. Transfer (송금)**
- **Cash exchange (현찰):** Spread is ~1.75% each way for USD. This is for physically receiving dollar bills.
- **Telegraphic transfer / account deposit (전신환):** Spread is ~1.0% each way. This is for holding USD in a foreign currency deposit account (외화예금).
- **우대율 applies to the spread**, not the mid-market rate. So 90% 우대 on a 1.75% spread means you pay only 0.175%.

### 3. Korean Securities Firms (증권사)

When you buy US stocks through a Korean brokerage, the broker converts KRW to USD. Most brokerages also allow **사전환전** (pre-conversion) where you convert KRW to USD in advance and hold it in your brokerage USD account.

| Brokerage | Standard FX Spread | 우대/환전 특판 | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **키움증권 (Kiwoom)** | ~0.25% | Periodic 0% spread events | Frequently runs "환전 우대" events, sometimes offering 0% spread. |
| **삼성증권 (Samsung)** | ~0.25% | Up to 95% 우대 → ~0.0125% | High 우대율 for overseas stock FX. |
| **미래에셋증권 (Mirae Asset)** | ~0.25% | Up to 90–95% 우대 → ~0.0125–0.025% | mStock app frequently provides 90%+ 우대. |

| **한국투자증권 (Korea Investment)** | ~0.25% | Up to 90–95%, periodic 100% 우대 events | Very popular for 해외주식; often runs 100% FX 우대 (= zero spread) for new accounts. |

**Important caveat:** USD held in a brokerage account is great for buying US stocks, but withdrawing to a bank account may incur additional transfer fees (typically 5,000–10,000 KRW).

### 6. Crypto Route (USDT) — NOT Recommended

| Step | Cost | Risk |
|---|---|---|
| Buy USDT on Upbit | ~0.05% trading fee + **kimchi premium** (3–8% typically) | Inflated KRW price |
| Transfer to global exchange | ~$1–5 network fee | Travel Rule regulatory risk |
| Sell USDT for USD | ~0.1% trading fee | Minimal |
| Withdraw USD to bank | ~$15–25 wire fee | Time delay |
| **Total effective cost** | **3–10%+ due to kimchi premium** | Regulatory, counterparty, volatility risk |

**Verdict:** The kimchi premium alone makes this the most expensive option. Not recommended for KRW→USD.

## Recommendations

### Best Overall: Korean Securities Firm with 우대 Event

**한국투자증권, 키움증권, or 미래에셋증권** during a promotional 환전 우대 event.

- **Cost: 0–0.025%** — the lowest possible
- Open a 해외주식 계좌 (overseas stock trading account)
- Use 사전환전 to convert KRW → USD at the promotional rate
- USD sits in the brokerage account; invest or withdraw later
- 100% 우대 events are frequent, especially for new accounts

### Best for Bank Account Holding: Hana Bank (하나은행) 90% 우대

- **Cost: ~0.10% (transfer) / ~0.175% (cash)**
- Use Hana mobile app for 90% 우대율
- Open a 외화보통예금 account and hold USD there
- Best mainstream bank option

### Best Fintech: Toss (토스)

- **Cost: ~0.20–0.35%**
- Extremely convenient, all in-app
- Good for moderate amounts

### Best for Sending USD Abroad: Wise or SentBe

- **Cost: ~0.3–0.6%**
- Far better than bank wire fees for international transfers

## Practical Playbook

1. **Open a 해외주식 account** at 한국투자증권 or 키움증권
2. **Wait for a 환전 우대 event** (check app promotions — they run very frequently)
3. **사전환전** your KRW to USD at 0% spread during the event
4. **Hold the USD** in the brokerage account (invest in US equities if desired)
5. If you need USD in a **bank account**, transfer out (small 5,000–10,000 KRW wire fee)
6. For **physical cash**, use Hana Bank's 90% 우대 cash exchange via mobile app

**Bottom line:** A Korean securities firm with a promotional 우대율 is the clear winner — potentially zero cost. For bank-based holding, Hana Bank's 90% 우대 is the best. Avoid standard bank rates and the crypto route.
